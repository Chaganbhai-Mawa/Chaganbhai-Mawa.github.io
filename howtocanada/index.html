Newcomer essentials

The first five things, in order.

Each one has its own guide, with the provincial variations spelled out.

Apply for a SIN

You need a Social Insurance Number before you can be paid. It's free, and Service Canada can issue it in person or online.

Open a bank account

Newcomer accounts usually waive fees for the first year. Bring your passport and immigration document.

Register for health coverage

Provincial coverage, and the waiting period before it starts, differ by province — check yours before you need care.

Sort your licence

Some provinces exchange a foreign licence directly; others require testing. The rules depend where you land.

Get a phone plan

Prepaid works without credit history. Postpaid usually needs a Canadian credit file or a deposit.
