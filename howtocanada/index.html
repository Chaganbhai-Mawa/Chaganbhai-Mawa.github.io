HowTo Canada turns the paperwork of arriving, studying, working and living in Canada into plain-language guides you can read offline, filtered to the province you actually live in.

What the app does

Read anywhere, offline

Guides are stored on the device. No connection needed once the app is installed, which matters on landing day and in transit.

Filter by province

Choose your province or territory and the guides adapt — health cards, licences and tax rules differ everywhere.

Twelve categories

Immigration, Students, Employment, Business, Taxes, Finance, Housing, Healthcare, Family, Transportation, Legal and Benefits.

CRS score estimate

Get a rough Comprehensive Ranking System figure and see which provincial streams may be worth a closer look.

Search and bookmarks

Search across every guide by keyword or province, and save the ones you're working through.

Newcomer essentials

A starting track for the first weeks: SIN, bank account, health card, driver's licence, phone plan.

Who it's for

Newcomers and prospective applicants, international students, temporary workers, and anyone helping family through a Canadian application. The writing assumes no prior knowledge and no immigration consultant on speed dial.

Important

HowTo Canada is an independent informational app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Government of Canada or any provincial or territorial government. It does not provide legal or immigration advice. Always confirm details on the official source, canada.ca, before making any decision.
