Privacy Policy

This policy explains what the HowTo Canada mobile application ("the App") collects, why, and what choices you have. It applies to the App on both Google Play and the Apple App Store.

Developer

Chaganbhai Keval Parmar

Privacy contact

Chagan123@yahoo.com

Summary

  • The App does not require an account, and you are never asked for your name, email address, phone number or immigration documents.
  • Guides, bookmarks, your province selection, recent searches and appearance settings are stored only on your device.
  • The App uses Google AdMob to display advertising and Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. These services collect limited technical and usage data as described below.
  • The developer does not sell personal information.

Information you provide

None is required. The App has no sign-up, no login and no user profile. If you choose to email the developer, you share whatever information you put in that message; it is used only to reply to you.

Information stored on your device only

The following stays in local storage on your phone and is not transmitted to the developer. Deleting the App removes it.

  • Selected province or territory
  • Bookmarked guides
  • Recent search terms
  • Dark mode and other display preferences
  • Any values you enter into the CRS score estimator, which are calculated on the device

Information collected automatically by third-party services

Google AdMob (advertising)

The App shows ads served by Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, AdMob may process device and usage information such as an advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID on Android, Identifier for Advertisers on iOS), IP address, device model, operating system, coarse location derived from IP address, and interactions with ads. Depending on your settings and region, ads may be personalised or non-personalised.

Google's handling of this data is described in the Google Privacy & Terms and in the AdMob documentation.

Google Firebase (analytics and stability)

The App uses Firebase services provided by Google, which may include Google Analytics for Firebase and Firebase Crashlytics. These collect aggregated, event-level information such as app opens, screens viewed, session length, approximate region, device model, operating system version, app version, language, an app-instance identifier, and crash and error diagnostics.

This data is used to understand which guides are useful, to fix crashes, and to improve the App. It is not used to identify you personally. See the Firebase Privacy and Security information for details.

How information is used

  • To display and measure advertising, which keeps the App free
  • To diagnose crashes and technical faults
  • To understand general usage patterns and improve content and features
  • To respond to messages you send to the contact address

The App does not use your information to make decisions about immigration applications and does not submit anything to any government body.

Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent or trade personal information, and does not share it with advertisers directly. Data is processed by the third-party providers named above acting as service providers, and may be disclosed if required by applicable law or valid legal process.

Your choices and controls

  • Advertising identifier. On Android you can reset or delete your Advertising ID and opt out of ad personalisation in Settings › Google › Ads. On iOS you can control app tracking in Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking, and the App will only request tracking permission where required.
  • Consent in the EEA, UK and Switzerland. Where required, the App presents a consent message for personalised advertising and you can change or withdraw your choice from within the App's settings.
  • Local data. Clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App removes everything held on the device.
  • Requests. To ask about the data associated with your use of the App, or to request deletion of information you sent by email, write to Chagan123@yahoo.com.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as Canada's PIPEDA, the EU and UK GDPR, or the California Consumer Privacy Act — including access, correction, deletion, and objection to certain processing. Requests can be sent to the contact address above and will be handled within the timeframes set by the applicable law.

Data retention

Information held on your device remains until you delete it or uninstall the App. Analytics, crash and advertising data are retained by Google according to their own retention policies and controls.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for a general adult audience, primarily people navigating Canadian immigration, study, work and settlement processes. It is not directed to children, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact the address above so it can be removed.

Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect information, including encrypted transmission to the third-party services named above. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

International transfers

The third-party services named above operate globally, so information they collect may be processed on servers outside your country, including in the United States, under the safeguards those providers describe in their own policies.

Links to other sites

The App links to official government websites such as canada.ca and to provincial resources. Those sites are governed by their own privacy policies, and the developer is not responsible for their content or practices.

Not a government service

HowTo Canada is an independent informational app. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Government of Canada or any provincial or territorial government, and it does not provide legal or immigration advice.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the App changes or as legal requirements evolve. The current version is always published at this address, and material changes will be signalled in the App.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to Chagan123@yahoo.com, or see the contact page.
